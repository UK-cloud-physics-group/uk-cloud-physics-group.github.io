UK Early-Career Cloud Physics Group

Calendar

This is a collection of upcoming events with potential application to cloud physics, including workshops and trainings that aren't cloud-focused. If you have a suggested addition, send it to uk-early-career-cloud-physics@gaggle.email or to one of the current chairs (Mathilde or Kallista).

If you have missed a registration deadline, it is always worth emailing the organisers and asking politely whether late registration is possible, especially for events like hackathons where the organisers want active participants.

May 18–22: Global to regional km-scale hackathon 2026, Exeter. Registration deadline was April 17th, but you can try emailing the conveners.
