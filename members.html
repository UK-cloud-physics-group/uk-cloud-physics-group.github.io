Optional fields

‘Research interests’ includes, but is not limited to, what you’ve worked on so far.
‘Tools/datasets worked with’ is intended to connect people for help: list items here where if someone’s just starting to work with it, they could ask you about your experience.
‘Miscellaneous’ is for whatever else you’d like to add.

If you'd like to change a detail, just resubmit the form.

Example:

Name: Kallista Angeloff
Contact details: kallista.angeloff [AT] physics.ox.ac.uk
Research interests: Aerosol–cloud interactions, Arctic aerosols, mixed-phase clouds, wildfire smoke aerosols, the various cloud versions of solar radiation modification
Tools/datasets worked with: general Unix/Linux, Python, the ICON-HAM and ICON-HAM-lite models, Fortran, Climate Data Operators (cdo), EarthCARE data
